How to Incubate a Chicken Egg artificially

Incubation period takes 21 days.

  • For Days 1-18, turning the eggs a minimum of 3 times a day so that every part of the egg can be incubated properly.
  • For Days 7-10, Candling the eggs are important, to see if the eggs are growing properly inside.
  • Days 18-21, Eggs will Pre-hatch into chicks and will take up most of the space in the egg. It is recommended to stop egg-turning at day 18 and to maintain a temperature of 100.5 F but increase humidity to 70%
